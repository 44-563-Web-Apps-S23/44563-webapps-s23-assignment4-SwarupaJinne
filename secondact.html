MRS. BAUER.

What you know?

LANE.

About the boss. [MRS. BAUER _looks distressed_.] Heard your friends across the street talkin'.

MRS. BAUER.

[_Bitterly._] Friends!

LANE.

Rotten trick to play on the boss, all right, puttin' that old maid up to get him pinched.

MRS. BAUER.

[_Absently._] Was she an old maid?
